Design 4: Neubrutalism

Bold High-Contrast Mode

Theme Concept

Uses stark neubrutalist elements: thick borders, flat hard black shadows, and bright color blocks. Conveys high energy, creative community expression, and high engagement.

Typography
  • Headers: Lexend (Bold display geometry)
  • Body: Space Grotesk (Quirky monospace tech vibe)
Color Palette
Key Visual Techniques

Thick black borders, hard non-blurry shadows, active element offset translations, flat geometric shapes, and tilted title plates.
