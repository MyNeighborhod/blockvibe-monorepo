Warm Earth Mode

Theme Concept

Brings a handcrafted, welcoming, sustainability-focused warmth to neighborhood digital spaces. Highlights community integration, nature, and shared social projects.

Typography

Color Palette
Key Visual Techniques

Wide layout gutters, soft leaf logo, non-harsh borders, rounded buttons, and warm non-pure-white backgrounds resembling paper fibers.
