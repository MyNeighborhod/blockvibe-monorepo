# Design 9: Pastel & Lavender (Soft Friendly Mode)

A friendly, warm, soft-pastel design system with rounded cards and bubble shadows.

## Design Approach
- **Theme Concept:** Highlights family, friendliness, approachability, and soft suburban neighborhood associations. Projects safety, warmth, and inclusion.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Background: Soft Lavender/Pink (`#faf5ff`)
  - Accent Color: Soft Purple (`#8b5cf6`)
  - Warm Highlight: Soft Peach/Orange (`#f97316` / `#ffedd5`)
  - Card Fill: Pure White (`#ffffff`)
  - Shadow: Soft Lavender Glow (`rgba(139, 92, 246, 0.08)`)
- **Typography:**
  - Header: *Quicksand* (Google Fonts) - A rounded display sans-serif, highly friendly and gentle.
  - Body: *Nunito* (Google Fonts) - Rounded sans-serif, soft and legible.
- **Key Visual Techniques:**
  - Very round card corners (`border-radius: 24px`).
  - Warm gradient backgrounds for cards and buttons.
  - Bubble-like, soft blurry shadows.
  - Playful, soft color blobs.
- **Target Tenants:** Suburban HOAs, family associations, parent-teacher-neighbor groups, local playgroups.
