# Design 4: Neubrutalism (Bold High-Contrast Mode)

A highly engaging, contemporary web aesthetic featuring thick black borders and vivid pastel overlays.

## Design Approach
- **Theme Concept:** Rejects smooth corporate curves in favor of high-energy, raw, expressive, and fun interactions.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Background: Pastel Yellow (`#fef9c3`)
  - Accent Color: Neon Cyan (`#22d3ee`)
  - Accent Orange: Bright Orange (`#f97316`)
  - Accent Pink: Punchy Rose (`#f43f5e`)
  - Card Border: Solid Heavy Black (`#000000`)
- **Typography:**
  - Header: *Lexend* (Google Fonts) - A bold, geometric sans with high visual impact.
  - Body: *Space Grotesk* (Google Fonts) - Clean tech font with quirky, blocky letterforms.
- **Key Visual Techniques:**
  - Hard, solid box-shadows (`box-shadow: 4px 4px 0px #000000`) without blur.
  - Thick black borders (`border: 3px solid #000000`).
  - Active button states that translate (`transform: translate(-2px, -2px)`) on hover and shift shadows.
  - Bold color block cards overlapping.
- **Target Tenants:** Art collectives, youth recreation associations, creative community spaces, modern tech-savvy blocks.
