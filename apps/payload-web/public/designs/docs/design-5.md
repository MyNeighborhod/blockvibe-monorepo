# Design 5: Editorial / Journalistic (Serif Newspaper Mode)

A premium, text-focused design system mimicking a high-end civic newsletter or newspaper.

## Design Approach
- **Theme Concept:** Focused on content, local stories, journalism, and civic newsletters. Projects authority, literacy, intellectual depth, and historic community roots.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Background: Newsprint Warm Gray (`#faf9f5`)
  - Accent Color: Deep Crimson Red (`#991b1b`)
  - Accent Charcoal: Dark Charcoal (`#1a1a1a`)
  - Card Fill: Transparent or matching paper fill (`#f5f4f0`)
  - Borders: Thin Slate Line (`#d1d5db`)
- **Typography:**
  - Header: *Lora* (Google Fonts) - A contemporary serif with brushed curves and high legibility.
  - Body: *Lora* (Google Fonts) - Consistent serif typography for an authentic newspaper look.
  - UI Accents: *DM Sans* (Google Fonts) - Clean sans-serif metadata.
- **Key Visual Techniques:**
  - Traditional newspaper column lines (`border-right`, `border-left`) separating grid sections.
  - Very light backgrounds with high-contrast text.
  - Large drop-caps or elegant quote layouts.
  - Text-decoration links with thick borders or animated underlines.
- **Target Tenants:** Neighborhood historic preservation boards, local journalism outlets, advocacy groups.
