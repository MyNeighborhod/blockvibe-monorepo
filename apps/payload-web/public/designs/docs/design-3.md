# Design 3: Organic Eco-Community (Warm Earth Mode)

An eco-friendly, warm design system conveying local cooperation and natural simplicity.

## Design Approach
- **Theme Concept:** Focus on community gardens, sustainability, agricultural collectives, and historic preservation. Feels warm, inviting, and handcrafted.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Background: Creamy Ivory (`#fdfbf7`)
  - Accent Color: Sage Green (`#6b705c`)
  - Accent Text: Deep Olive/Forest Green (`#3f4238`)
  - Card Fill: Warm Off-White (`#f5f2eb`)
  - Borders: Warm Earth Tan (`#dddbd1`)
- **Typography:**
  - Header: *Playfair Display* (Google Fonts) - A timeless, warm serif with high elegance.
  - Body: *Lato* (Google Fonts) - Warm sans-serif with friendly curves.
- **Key Visual Techniques:**
  - Generous padding and wide, breathable margins.
  - Leaf/organic icon structures and soft rounded border corners (`border-radius: 20px`).
  - Warm, non-white paper backgrounds.
  - Soft serif headlines that feel human and literary.
- **Target Tenants:** Urban community gardens, organic farming collectives, historic preservation societies.
