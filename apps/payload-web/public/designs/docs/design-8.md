# Design 8: Swiss Design / Typographic Minimalist

A highly structured, grid-based layout focused on typographic scale and bold red/black/white contrasts.

## Design Approach
- **Theme Concept:** Inspired by mid-century modernist typography (Helvetica, Emil Ruder). Clear, bold, functional layout that uses asymmetrical balance and large fonts rather than icons or decorations.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Background: Stark White (`#ffffff`)
  - Accent Red: Swiss Red (`#dc2626`)
  - Dark Accent: Pitch Black (`#111111`)
  - Borders: Thin Black Lines (`#111111`)
- **Typography:**
  - Header & Body: *Arimo* (Google Fonts) - A clean, neo-grotesque sans-serif with strict geometric proportions, standing in for Helvetica.
- **Key Visual Techniques:**
  - Strict modular grid layouts.
  - Large, heavy headings (e.g. `font-weight: 800`) directly next to very small, neat body text.
  - Generous asymmetrical negative space.
  - Simple horizontal line dividers (`border-bottom: 2px solid #111111`).
- **Target Tenants:** Design collectives, modern architectural co-ops, premium civic coalitions, functional planning boards.
