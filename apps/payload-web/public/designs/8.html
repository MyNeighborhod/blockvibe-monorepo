Design 8: Swiss Design

Typographic Minimalist

Theme Concept

Inspired by mid-century modernist typography (Swiss Style). Strict grid placement, bold typography scale contrasts, and stark crimson color overlays. Focused entirely on clean layouts.

Typography
  • Headers & Body: Arimo (Structured geometric sans-serif that mimics Helvetica Neue)
Color Palette
Key Visual Techniques

Rigid asymmetrical grids, oversized sans headers, thin line spacers, high contrasts, and uppercase menu utilities.
